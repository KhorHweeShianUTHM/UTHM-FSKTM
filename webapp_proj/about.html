About

We are a coffee shop cafe' business by Gastro Scholar and developed by Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET) technopreneur for Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia (UTHM) students. Since its inception in 2019, Caffeine & Calories (C&C) have been a focus.

Why Choose Our Coffee Shop

1. Convenience

At our coffee shop, convenience is key. We understand the fast-paced nature of campus life, and we've tailored our services to seamlessly fit into your schedule. With strategically located outlets around the school, we ensure that a delicious cup of coffee is always within reach. Whether you're rushing between classes or seeking a cozy spot for a study break, our accessible locations make your coffee fix effortlessly convenient.

2. Affordable

We believe that quality coffee shouldn't come with a hefty price tag. Our commitment to affordability means you can enjoy premium coffee without burning a hole in your pocket. We source high-quality beans at reasonable prices, allowing us to offer a range of wallet-friendly options. Your daily caffeine boost shouldn't break the bank, and at our coffee shop, we make sure it doesn't.

3. Open Kitchen

Transparency is at the heart of our coffee-making process. Step into our open kitchen, and witness the magic as skilled baristas craft your favorite brews. From selecting the finest beans to the precise art of brewing, our open kitchen concept invites you to experience the journey of your coffee firsthand. We take pride in sharing our passion for coffee, ensuring that every cup is made with care, skill, and a touch of artistry.

4. Good Service

Experience hospitality in every cup with our commitment to exceptional service. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff is dedicated to ensuring your coffee experience is nothing short of delightful. From personalized recommendations to a warm ambiance, we prioritize customer satisfaction. At our coffee shop, good service is not just a promise – it's a fundamental part of the coffee-drinking experience, making every visit a pleasure.

Choose our coffee shop for the perfect blend of convenience, affordability, and the engaging experience of an open kitchen — where your coffee is more than a beverage; it's a crafted moment designed just for you.
